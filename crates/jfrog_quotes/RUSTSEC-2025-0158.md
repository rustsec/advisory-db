# `jfrog_quotes` was removed from crates.io for malicious code

`jfrog_quotes` was part of a campaign that attempted to exfiltrate
environmental data from the host.

The malicious crate had 1 version published in January 2025, and had no
evidence of actual usage. This crate had no dependencies on crates.io.
