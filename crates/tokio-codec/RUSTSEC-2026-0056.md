# `tokio-codec` is unmaintained

The [`tokio-codec`](https://crates.io/crates/tokio-codec) crate is unmaintained.
It was part of the Tokio 0.1 ecosystem and has been superseded by the [`tokio-util`](https://crates.io/crates/tokio-util) crate.
