# Fragile bounds check when sampling from image

A read of pixels was coded as modifying coordinates to lie within the image
bounds. It would calculate a coordinate by adding a constant to an input and
taking the minimum of the resulting coordinate and 'dimension - 1'. This would
not protect against malicious inputs that could overflow the addition. .
Subsequently to the tricked bounds check the image could then be sampled at
multiple, differently calculated coordinates exceeding the bounds.
